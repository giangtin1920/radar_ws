Short Range Radar

Introduction

This use case provides the sensing configuration for a Short Range Radar which allows the estimation of the position (in the azimuth plane) and the velocity of objects in its field of view up to 84 meters. This configuration is based on TI's 80m-Range Object Detection Reference Design and the reader is encouraged to look at the design webpage for more details.

2. Special considerations, Limitations etc

The Chirp Configuration used in TI's 80m-Range Object Detection Reference Design assumes a fixed sampling rate of 5 Msps whereas the Sensing Estimator calculates the minimum required sampling rate based on the maximum beat frequency. Because of this, the chirp configuration presented here does not exactly match the one used in the TI Design.
