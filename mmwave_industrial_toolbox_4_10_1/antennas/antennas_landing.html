[[y! Angular Resolution vs Angular Accuracy Angular resolution is defined as how far apart two objects need to be (in degrees) for them to be detected as different/separate objects. It is different from ‘Angular Accuracy’ which is how accurately the angle of an object is detected. ]] # TI mmWave Antennas The mmWave Antennas collection is an easy-to-search repository of different mmWave antennas put together by the TI mmWave team. TI-designed antennas are available to get started reusing the stack-up and layout for the same antenna for your application. Each antenna design is validated as a part of a board design. Depending on your application, you can choose an antenna from the table below.
